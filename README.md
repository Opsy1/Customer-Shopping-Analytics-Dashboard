# Customer Shopping Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
The Customer Shopping Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze retail shopping data and transform it into meaningful business insights. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer demographics, product performance, and shopping mall performance. 
Its purpose is to help decision-makers understand customer purchasing behavior, monitor business performance, and identify opportunities for growth through data-driven insights.

## Tech Stack
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation before analysis.
* **Power BI** – Dashboard development, data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive visualizations.

## Data Source
The dataset was provided by HieLite Academy as part of a hands-on Power BI training project.

## Features / Highlights

### Business Problem
Retail businesses generate large volumes of transaction data every day. While the data contains valuable information, it can be difficult for managers to quickly understand overall performance, customer buying patterns, product demand, and the performance of different shopping locations.

* Without an interactive reporting system, answering questions such as:
* Which shopping mall generates the highest revenue?
* Which customer groups contribute the most sales?
* Which product categories perform best?
* What payment methods do customers prefer?
requires significant manual effort and analysis.
This dashboard addresses these challenges by presenting business information in a clear, interactive, and easy-to-understand format.

### Project Goal

The primary goal of this project was to build an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables business users to:
* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Understand customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate product category performance.
* Compare shopping mall performance.
* Support faster, data-driven business decisions.

### Business Impact
The dashboard indicates a business with strong revenue performance, a loyal core customer demographic, and several high-performing product categories and shopping locations. However, opportunities exist to increase repeat purchases, improve the performance of weaker product categories, and better understand seasonal sales fluctuations.
